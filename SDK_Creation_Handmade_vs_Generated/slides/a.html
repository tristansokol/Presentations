And at Square we a ton of different teams working on our APIs, but only one team working and focused on how developers will use all of this functionality. When API requests come in, they hit our team and then we route them to the appropriate team or service. That means when it comes time to create or update our sdks, there are a ton of cooks in the kitchen. And those cooks really do not like making SDKs. So our team help take those differening opinions and turn them into a more consistant developer experience.
